Job search tips for India

Use multiple platforms simultaneously

Each portal has different strengths. Naukri has the most listings, LinkedIn gets you recruiter attention, Internshala is best for freshers, and Wellfound is best for startups. Apply on at least 3 platforms for maximum coverage.

Optimize your Naukri profile for keywords

Naukri's search algorithm matches keywords. Include specific technologies, tools, and certifications in your profile. "React.js developer with 3 years experience in TypeScript and AWS" will get more recruiter views than "Software Engineer".

LinkedIn activity matters more than your resume

Post regularly about your work, comment on industry posts, and engage with hiring managers. Active profiles get 3x more recruiter messages. Join relevant groups and follow companies you want to work for.
