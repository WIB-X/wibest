Tips for property buyers in India

Always verify RERA registration

Check the project's RERA number on your state's RERA website (e.g., rera.karnataka.gov.in, rera.tn.gov.in). Unregistered projects are illegal to sell. RERA protects you from delayed possession and construction defects.

Compare across multiple portals

The same property can be listed at different prices on 99acres, MagicBricks, and Housing.com. Some listings are inflated. Always visit the property physically and negotiate directly with the builder or owner.

Factor in hidden costs

The listed price is not the final cost. Add: stamp duty (5-7%), registration charges (1%), GST on under-construction flats (5%), brokerage (1-2%), parking, maintenance deposit, and interior costs. Budget 15-20% above the listed price.

Use NoBroker for rentals

Traditional brokers charge 1-2 months rent as brokerage. NoBroker eliminates this entirely. For a Rs 25,000/month rental, you save Rs 25,000-50,000. They also offer packers and movers, painting, and legal services.
