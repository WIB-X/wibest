Price comparison tips

Check all stores before buying

The same product can vary by Rs 1,000-5,000 between Amazon and Flipkart depending on the day. Croma and Reliance Digital sometimes offer additional exchange bonuses not available online. Always check at least 2-3 stores.

Wait for sale events

Amazon Great Indian Festival (October), Flipkart Big Billion Days (October), Amazon Prime Day (July), and Republic Day sales (January) offer the biggest discounts - typically 15-40% off on electronics. If your purchase is not urgent, wait for the next sale.

Use bank offers and credit card discounts

Amazon often gives 10% instant discount with SBI/ICICI credit cards. Flipkart does the same with Axis Bank. These stack with existing sale prices. A Rs 50,000 laptop can effectively become Rs 42,000-45,000 with the right combination.

Check warranty and seller

Always buy from the official brand store or "Fulfilled by Amazon/Flipkart Assured" sellers. Third-party sellers may offer lower prices but warranty claims become difficult. For Apple products, verify on checkcoverage.apple.com after purchase.
