Frequently asked questions

What is a GaN charger? +
GaN (Gallium Nitride) chargers use a newer semiconductor that is more efficient than silicon. They deliver the same power in a much smaller size, generate less heat, and charge faster.
Which GaN charger is best for iPhone? +
The Apple 20W USB-C (Rs 1,749) is safest. For multi-device, the Anker 735 65W (Rs 3,499) charges iPhone plus MacBook simultaneously.
