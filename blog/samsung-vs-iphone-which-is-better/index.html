Frequently asked questions

Which is better Samsung or iPhone in India? +
Samsung offers better value at every price point, more variety (Rs 8K-1.8L), wider service network (2000+ centers). iPhone offers better video, longer updates (6+ years), better resale value, and tighter ecosystem with Mac/iPad.
Why is iPhone expensive in India? +
Import duties (22%) and GST (18%) make iPhones 20-40% costlier than US prices. iPhone 16 costs Rs 79,900 in India vs roughly Rs 66,000 equivalent in the US.
