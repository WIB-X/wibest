Frequently asked questions

Which monitor size is best for WFH? +
27 inches is the sweet spot for WFH. Big enough for split-screen productivity but not overwhelming. If desk space is limited, 24 inches works well. For coding and design, 27-inch 4K is ideal.
Is 4K monitor worth it for work? +
For document work and browsing, 1080p at 24 inches or 1440p at 27 inches is sufficient. 4K is worth it only for photo/video editing, design work, or if you connect a MacBook (macOS scales beautifully at 4K).
