Our top pick: Samsung Galaxy M34 5G

The M34 offers the best balance of everything - massive 6000mAh battery that lasts 2 days, vibrant AMOLED display, reliable Exynos processor, and Samsung's promise of 4 years of security updates. The 120Hz refresh rate makes scrolling smooth. It handles most games at medium settings comfortably.

Best for camera: Redmi Note 13

If camera quality is your priority, the 108MP sensor on the Redmi Note 13 captures significantly more detail than 50MP rivals. Night mode is also impressive for the price. However, battery life is average compared to the M34.

Best for gaming: Poco X6 Neo

The Dimensity 6080 with its better GPU handles games like BGMI and Genshin Impact at higher settings than other phones in this range. If you game frequently, this is the pick.

What to look for in a phone under 15000

At this budget, prioritize these features: a good AMOLED display (avoid IPS unless battery is priority), at least 6GB RAM and 128GB storage, 5G connectivity, a 5000mAh or larger battery, and a brand with good after-sales service in India (Samsung, Xiaomi, and Motorola have the widest service networks).

Avoid spending extra on flashy specs like 108MP cameras if the sensor quality is mediocre. A well-tuned 50MP camera often takes better photos than a poorly optimized 108MP one. Read reviews and check sample photos before deciding.

Frequently asked questions

Which is the best phone under 15000 in India 2026? +
The Samsung Galaxy M34 5G is the best overall phone under 15000 in India. It offers a 6000mAh battery, 120Hz AMOLED display, Exynos 1280 processor, and 50MP camera. For gaming, the Poco X6 Neo with Dimensity 6080 is a better choice.
Is 5G important in a phone under 15000? +
Yes, in 2026 most phones under 15000 come with 5G. With Jio and Airtel expanding 5G coverage to tier-2 cities, buying a 5G phone future-proofs your purchase for the next 2-3 years.
Which phone has the best camera under 15000? +
The Redmi Note 13 has the best camera in this budget with a 108MP primary sensor. For video recording, the Motorola G84 performs better with OIS (Optical Image Stabilization).
How long will a phone under 15000 last? +
A good phone under 15000 should last 2-3 years with regular use. Look for at least 6GB RAM, 128GB storage, and a brand that provides 2+ years of software updates like Samsung or Motorola.
