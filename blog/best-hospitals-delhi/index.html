For cancer: Rajiv Gandhi Cancer Institute (RGCI)

Dedicated cancer hospital with 302 beds, NABH and NABL accredited. One of the largest BMT centres in India. Focuses exclusively on oncology, which means deeper expertise than multi-speciality hospitals.

For heart: Fortis Escorts Heart Institute

Founded by Dr. S.C. Manchanda, this is where Indian cardiac surgery was pioneered. Over 40 years of cardiac expertise. 4.6 rating. Their outcomes match the best in the world at a fraction of the cost.

Frequently asked questions

Which is the best hospital in Delhi? +
AIIMS Delhi is the best overall hospital in Delhi with 4.8 rating, 2,478 beds, and excellence in virtually every speciality. Among private hospitals, Indraprastha Apollo and Max Saket are the top choices for multi-speciality treatment. For heart surgery, Fortis Escorts is legendary.
Which is the best private hospital in Delhi NCR? +
Medanta Gurugram (1,500 beds, JCI accredited) and Max Super Speciality Saket are considered the best private hospitals in Delhi NCR. Medanta is particularly strong in liver transplant, cardiac surgery, and robotics. Max Saket excels in oncology and neurosciences.
