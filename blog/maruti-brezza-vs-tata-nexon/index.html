Frequently asked questions

Which is better Brezza or Nexon? +
Nexon for safety (5-star vs 4-star GNCAP), ground clearance, diesel option. Brezza for mileage (20+ kmpl vs 17 kmpl), service network (4000+ vs 1100), resale value, and CNG option.
Which has better mileage? +
Brezza: 20-21 kmpl petrol, 26+ km/kg CNG. Nexon: 17-18 kmpl petrol, 23-24 kmpl diesel. Brezza wins on fuel efficiency.
