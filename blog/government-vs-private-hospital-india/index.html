Frequently asked questions

Are government hospitals better? +
Government hospitals have experienced doctors (professors), lower costs, and no unnecessary tests. Private hospitals have shorter waits, cleaner facilities, and better nursing. For serious surgeries, government teaching hospitals often have better outcomes due to volume.
Why are government hospitals free? +
Funded by taxpayers through government budgets. Under Ayushman Bharat, treatment up to Rs 5 lakh/year is free for eligible families.
