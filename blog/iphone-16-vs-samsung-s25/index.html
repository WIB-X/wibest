Frequently asked questions

Which is better iPhone 16 or Samsung S25? +
iPhone 16 is better for Apple ecosystem users, video recording, and resale value. Samsung S25 is better for 120Hz display, Galaxy AI features, better zoom, and Android customization. Both are excellent flagships.
