Best Places to Visit in India in April (2026) - Beat the Heat

By WIB TeamMarch 2026

April means brutal 40C+ heat in Indian plains. Head to the hills or coast. Here are the best destinations.

Top pick: Ooty or Kodaikanal

Both are perfectly cool when plains are scorching. Ooty for families (toy train, gardens). Kodaikanal for couples (lake, quiet walks). From Bangalore, Ooty is closer. From Chennai, both are similar distance.

Frequently asked questions

Where to travel in India in April? +
Hill stations: Ooty (12-25C), Kodaikanal (12-22C), Munnar (15-25C), Yercaud (14-26C) in South India. Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala in North. Ladakh opens late April. Avoid plains - they will be 40C+.
Is April good for Ooty? +
Yes, April is one of the best months for Ooty. Temperature stays 12-25C. It is peak season so book hotels in advance.
