15 Best Weekend Trips from Chennai (2026) - With Distances and Budget

By WIB TeamUpdated 2026-03-279 min read

Chennai residents are blessed with diverse getaway options within 500 km - beaches, hills, temples, heritage towns, and wildlife. Here are the 15 best weekend trips you can take from Chennai, organized by distance.

Under 100 km (day trip possible)

1. Mahabalipuram - 55 km (1.5 hrs)

UNESCO shore temples, Arjuna's Penance rock carvings, Krishna's Butterball, and a beautiful beach. The ECR drive is pleasant. Best for: history lovers, families, photography. Budget: Rs 1,500-3,000 for a day trip. Travel: Car or bike on ECR.

2. Kanchipuram - 75 km (2 hrs)

The City of Thousand Temples. Visit Kailasanathar Temple, Ekambareswarar Temple, and buy authentic Kanchipuram silk sarees directly from weavers. Best for: temple lovers, silk shopping. Budget: Rs 1,000-2,500 for a day. Travel: Car or bus from Koyambedu.

3. Tada Falls (Ubbalamadugu) - 90 km (2 hrs)

A hidden waterfall near Sullurupeta in Andhra Pradesh. The 2 km trek through the forest is the highlight. Best visited during or just after monsoon (Aug-Nov). Best for: trekkers, nature lovers. Budget: Rs 500-1,000 per person. Travel: Car only (no public transport to falls).

100-200 km (overnight recommended)

4. Tirupati - 135 km (3 hrs)

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, one of the most visited religious sites in the world. Book darshan tickets online in advance on tirumala.org. Best for: pilgrimage, spiritual experience. Budget: Rs 2,000-5,000 for overnight. Travel: Car, bus from Koyambedu, or train to Tirupati.

5. Vellore - 140 km (2.5 hrs)

Vellore Fort (16th century), Golden Temple (Sripuram), and CMC Hospital for medical tourism. The Golden Temple is stunning at night. Best for: history, architecture. Budget: Rs 2,000-4,000 overnight. Travel: Train to Katpadi or drive on NH46.

6. Pondicherry - 170 km (3.5 hrs via ECR)

The most popular weekend trip from Chennai. French Quarter with yellow colonial buildings, Promenade Beach seafront, Auroville Matrimandir, surfing at Serenity Beach, and incredible French-Tamil fusion food. The ECR drive is half the experience. Best for: everyone. Budget: Rs 3,000-8,000 for 2 days. Travel: Drive or ride on ECR - avoid NH for the scenic route.

200-350 km (weekend trip)

7. Yelagiri Hills - 230 km (4 hrs)

Peaceful hill station with Punganoor Lake, Nature Park, and paragliding. Much less crowded than Ooty or Kodaikanal. Temperature stays 20-28C year round. Best for: families wanting a quiet hill escape. Budget: Rs 3,000-6,000 for 2 days. Travel: Drive via Vaniyambadi.

8. Chidambaram and Pichavaram - 240 km (5 hrs)

Nataraja Temple (one of the Pancha Bhoota Stalams) plus the stunning Pichavaram Mangrove Forest boat ride. The mangroves are the second largest in the world. Best for: temple lovers, nature, boating. Budget: Rs 2,500-5,000 for 2 days. Travel: Drive on ECR via Cuddalore.

9. Kumbakonam - 280 km (5.5 hrs)

Temple town with 188 temples. Visit Airavatesvara Temple (UNESCO), Kumbeswarar Temple, and Darasuram. The filter coffee here is legendary. Best for: heritage, temple architecture. Budget: Rs 2,500-5,000 for 2 days. Travel: Train to Kumbakonam or drive via Trichy.

10. Chettinad - 340 km (6 hrs)

Palatial mansions of the Chettiar community. The architecture is incredible - each mansion took decades to build with materials from around the world. Chettinad cuisine (spicy, authentic) is a food lover's paradise. Best for: architecture, food lovers. Budget: Rs 4,000-8,000 for 2 days. Travel: Drive via Trichy and Sivaganga.

350-500 km (extended weekend)

11. Rameshwaram - 530 km (8 hrs)

The Pamban Bridge crossing is breathtaking. Ramanathaswamy Temple, Dhanushkodi ghost town, and APJ Abdul Kalam Memorial. Best for: pilgrimage, unique landscapes. Budget: Rs 4,000-7,000 for 2-3 days. Travel: Train is best (crosses Pamban Bridge). Take Friday night train, return Sunday night.

12. Kodaikanal - 530 km (9 hrs)

Princess of Hill Stations. Lake, Coaker's Walk, Pine Forest, Pillar Rocks. Take Friday evening bus/drive, full Saturday and Sunday exploring. Best for: couples, nature lovers. Budget: Rs 4,000-10,000 for 2-3 days. Travel: Drive via Dindigul or overnight bus.

13. Ooty - 560 km (10 hrs)

Queen of Hill Stations. Toy train, botanical gardens, tea estates, Doddabetta Peak. Needs at least 2 full days. Best for: families, tea lovers. Budget: Rs 5,000-12,000 for 2-3 days. Travel: Drive via Salem-Mettupalayam, or train to Mettupalayam + toy train.

14. Munnar - 580 km (10 hrs)

Kerala's most beautiful hill station. Tea gardens as far as the eye can see, Eravikulam National Park, Top Station views. Best for: nature, photography. Budget: Rs 5,000-10,000 for 2-3 days. Travel: Drive via Madurai-Theni or Kochi route.

15. Coorg - 530 km (9 hrs via Mysore)

Coffee country in Karnataka. Abbey Falls, Raja's Seat viewpoint, coffee plantation stays. The drive through Mysore is pleasant. Best for: coffee lovers, couples, nature. Budget: Rs 5,000-12,000 for 2-3 days. Travel: Drive via Bangalore-Mysore or direct via Salem-Mysore.

Frequently asked questions

What are the best weekend trips from Chennai? +
The best weekend trips from Chennai are: Pondicherry (170 km, 3.5 hrs), Mahabalipuram (55 km, 1.5 hrs), Yelagiri Hills (230 km, 4 hrs), Vellore (140 km, 2.5 hrs), Tirupati (135 km, 3 hrs), Auroville (165 km, 3.5 hrs), Kanchipuram (75 km, 2 hrs), and Chidambaram (240 km, 5 hrs).
Which is the nearest hill station from Chennai? +
Yelagiri Hills is the nearest proper hill station from Chennai at 230 km (4 hours drive). It is less crowded and more peaceful than Ooty or Kodaikanal. For a shorter trip, Tada Falls near Sullurupeta (90 km) offers a nature escape in under 2 hours.
Is Pondicherry good for a weekend trip from Chennai? +
Yes, Pondicherry is the most popular weekend trip from Chennai at just 170 km (3-3.5 hrs via ECR). The East Coast Road drive itself is scenic. You can explore French Quarter, Promenade Beach, and Auroville in a weekend. Budget: Rs 3,000-8,000 per person for 2 days including travel, stay, and food.
