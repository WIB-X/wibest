Frequently asked questions

Which is the best hospital in Bangalore? +
Narayana Health City is the best overall with 3,200 beds and JCI accreditation. For mental health NIMHANS is unmatched. For orthopedics Sparsh Hospital specializes exclusively.
Best government hospital in Bangalore? +
NIMHANS for neurology, Jayadeva for cardiac, and Kidwai Memorial for cancer are the top three government hospitals.
