Best overall: Samsung Galaxy Watch FE

This is the sweet spot. Wear OS means you get Google Maps, Spotify, WhatsApp on your wrist. Samsung's BioActive sensor measures heart rate, blood oxygen, body composition (fat/muscle/water), and even ECG. Works with any Android phone. At Rs 14,999, nothing else comes close for health accuracy.

Best budget: Noise ColorFit Pro 5

If your budget is under Rs 3,000, Noise is the safest choice. The AMOLED display is sharp, Bluetooth calling works well, and 7-day battery means you charge once a week. Health data is approximate but good enough for general fitness awareness.

Frequently asked questions

Which is the best smartwatch in India? +
For most people, the Samsung Galaxy Watch FE at Rs 14,999 is the best smartwatch in India. It has accurate health tracking (heart rate, SpO2, sleep, body composition), Google Wear OS with apps, and works with any Android phone. For iPhones, the Apple Watch SE at Rs 29,900 is the only good option.
Are cheap smartwatches under 2000 worth buying? +
Budget smartwatches from Noise, Fire-Boltt, and boAt (Rs 1,500-3,000) are good for notifications, step counting, and basic heart rate. However, their health data accuracy is significantly lower than Samsung or Apple watches. They are fine as a first smartwatch but not for serious health monitoring.
