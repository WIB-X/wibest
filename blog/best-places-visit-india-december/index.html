Frequently asked questions

Best place in India in December? +
Goa for beaches and Christmas. Jaisalmer for desert camping. Kerala backwaters for houseboats. Rann of Kutch for the white desert festival.
Is December good to visit India? +
Excellent. North India has pleasant winter, South India is warm and dry, Rajasthan is cool. Only extreme north (Ladakh, Spiti) is inaccessible.
