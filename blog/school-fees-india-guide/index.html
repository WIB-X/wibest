Frequently asked questions

How much do schools cost in India? +
Government: free to Rs 5,000/year. Budget private: Rs 15,000-50,000. Good CBSE: Rs 50,000-2,00,000. Top ICSE: Rs 1,00,000-3,00,000. International IB: Rs 3,00,000-15,00,000 per year.
What are hidden school costs? +
Beyond tuition: admission fees (Rs 10,000-1,00,000 one-time), books and uniforms (Rs 5,000-15,000/year), transport (Rs 15,000-40,000/year), activities (Rs 5,000-20,000/year). These add 30-50% to tuition.
