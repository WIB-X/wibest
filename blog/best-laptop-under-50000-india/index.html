Frequently asked questions

Which is the best laptop under 50000 in India? +
The Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 3 with AMD Ryzen 5 is the best overall laptop under 50000. It offers excellent performance, good battery life (8+ hours), a decent display, and reliable build quality. For gaming, the ASUS VivoBook with Ryzen 5 and integrated Radeon graphics is a better choice.
Is 8GB RAM enough in 2026? +
8GB RAM is the minimum for comfortable use in 2026. It handles browsing, Office, video calls, and light editing. However, if you plan to keep the laptop for 3+ years, open many browser tabs, or do any coding/design work, look for 16GB RAM options. Some laptops under 50000 now come with 16GB.
Which laptop brand has the best service in India? +
HP and Lenovo have the widest service networks in India with centers in most tier-2 and tier-3 cities. Dell also has excellent service but availability varies. ASUS and Acer service is reliable in metros but limited in smaller cities.
