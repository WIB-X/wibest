For cancer: Tata Memorial Hospital

Simply the best cancer hospital in India. Government-run, so treatment is subsidized for those who cannot afford private care. Their BMT and surgical oncology outcomes match global standards. Wait times can be long due to demand from across India.

Frequently asked questions

Which is the best hospital in Mumbai? +
For cancer, Tata Memorial Hospital is the best in India (4.7 rating, government, affordable). For multi-speciality private care, Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital (JCI accredited) and HN Reliance Foundation Hospital are the top choices. Lilavati and Hinduja are excellent trust hospitals.
Which is the cheapest good hospital in Mumbai? +
KEM Hospital (government, free/subsidized), Tata Memorial for cancer (subsidized), and Sion Hospital are the most affordable quality options. Among private hospitals, Wockhardt and Seven Hills offer comparatively lower rates than Kokilaben or Lilavati.
