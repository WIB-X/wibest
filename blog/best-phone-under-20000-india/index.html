Our top pick: Samsung Galaxy A35 5G

The A35 is the most well-rounded phone under 20000. IP67 water resistance means it survives rain and accidental spills. The AMOLED display is vivid and bright. Samsung guarantees 4 generations of Android updates, meaning this phone stays current until 2030. Camera quality with OIS is reliable in all conditions.

Best for gaming: Poco X6

The Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 is the most powerful chipset in this price range. It handles BGMI at extreme settings and Genshin Impact at high quality. If gaming matters, this is your pick. The trade-off is software quality, which is not as clean as Samsung or OnePlus.

Best battery: iQOO Z9 Turbo

6000mAh battery with 144Hz display and a powerful Dimensity 8300. This phone easily lasts 2 days for most users. The 144Hz refresh rate is the smoothest in this price range.

Frequently asked questions

Which is the best phone under 20000 in 2026? +
The Samsung Galaxy A35 5G is the best overall phone under 20000. It has an excellent AMOLED display, IP67 water resistance (rare at this price), good cameras, and 4 years of updates. For gaming, the Poco X6 with Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 is the better choice.
Is OnePlus Nord CE 4 Lite worth buying? +
Yes, the Nord CE 4 Lite offers clean OxygenOS, fast charging (80W SUPERVOOC), and a smooth display. However, camera quality is average compared to Samsung A35 and the processor is not as powerful as Poco X6.
Which phone under 20000 has the best camera? +
The Samsung Galaxy A35 5G has the most versatile camera system with OIS on the main sensor, good night mode, and reliable video stabilization. The Realme 12 Pro has a telephoto lens for zoom shots which is unique at this price.
