Ooty vs Kodaikanal - Which Hill Station is Better? (Complete Comparison)

By WIB TeamUpdated 2026-03-278 min read

Ooty and Kodaikanal are the two most popular hill stations in Tamil Nadu and among the top weekend getaways from Chennai, Bangalore, Coimbatore, and Madurai. Both offer cool weather, scenic views, and an escape from the plains heat. But they have very different vibes.

Choose Ooty if:

You are travelling with family and children. You want to ride the Nilgiri Mountain Railway (toy train). You love tea plantations and botanical gardens. You are coming from Bangalore or Coimbatore (closer). You want more hotel and restaurant choices.

Choose Kodaikanal if:

You are a couple looking for a romantic getaway. You prefer fewer crowds and a more laid-back vibe. You enjoy trekking and nature walks. You are coming from Madurai or Chennai (closer). You are on a tighter budget.

Top things to do

Ooty highlights

  • Nilgiri Mountain Railway toy train (UNESCO heritage, the highlight of any Ooty trip)
  • Government Botanical Gardens (spread across 22 hectares, 1000+ plant species)
  • Doddabetta Peak (highest point in Nilgiris at 2,637m, panoramic views)
  • Tea Museum and factory tour (learn how Nilgiri tea is made, free tasting)
  • Ooty Lake boating (pedal boats and row boats, pleasant in evening)
  • Pykara Falls and boating (20 km from Ooty, beautiful waterfall)

Kodaikanal highlights

  • Kodaikanal Lake (star-shaped, boating and cycling around the lake)
  • Coaker's Walk (1 km cliffside path with valley views, morning mist is magical)
  • Pillar Rocks (three granite pillars rising 122m, viewpoint)
  • Dolphin's Nose viewpoint (3 km trek with stunning valley panorama)
  • Pine Forest (tall pine trees, carpet of pine needles, very photogenic)
  • Bryant Park (botanical garden near the lake, flower shows)

Frequently asked questions

Which is better Ooty or Kodaikanal? +
Ooty is better for families with children (botanical gardens, toy train, lake), tea estate visits, and a more developed tourism infrastructure. Kodaikanal is better for couples (more peaceful, romantic lake, quiet walks) and trekkers (Dolphin's Nose, Pillar Rocks trails). Both are excellent for weekend trips from Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, or Kerala.
Which is colder Ooty or Kodaikanal? +
Kodaikanal is generally colder than Ooty, especially at night. Kodaikanal ranges from 8-20C in winter and 12-22C in summer. Ooty ranges from 5-21C in winter and 12-25C in summer. Both require jackets in the evening year-round.
How far is Ooty from Kodaikanal? +
Ooty to Kodaikanal is approximately 260 km by road and takes about 7-8 hours to drive. There is no direct train. The route passes through Coimbatore and Palani. Most travellers visit them on separate trips rather than combining both.
Which is cheaper Ooty or Kodaikanal? +
Kodaikanal is generally cheaper than Ooty. Hotels in Kodaikanal start from Rs 500/night (budget) compared to Rs 800+ in Ooty. Food is similarly priced. Ooty's toy train and botanical garden have entry fees. However, Ooty has more mid-range and luxury options if budget is not a constraint.
