Hyundai Creta vs Tata Nexon (2026) - Which SUV Should You Buy?

By WIB TeamUpdated 2026-03-278 min read

The Hyundai Creta and Tata Nexon are two of the best-selling SUVs in India. While they are in slightly different size categories (Creta is a compact SUV, Nexon is a sub-compact SUV), they compete directly in the Rs 10-15 lakh range where most Indian buyers shop.

Buy the Hyundai Creta if:

You want a more spacious cabin for family road trips. Premium interior finish matters to you. You do long highway drives frequently (better NVH and comfort). You want the widest range of engine options including turbo petrol. Resale value is important (Hyundai resells well in India).

Buy the Tata Nexon if:

Safety is your top priority (5-star vs 3-star GNCAP). You want the best value for money with top features at lower price. You drive mostly in the city (higher ground clearance, compact for parking). You are considering the EV option (Nexon EV is excellent). You want better fuel efficiency.

Frequently asked questions

Which is better Creta or Nexon? +
The Hyundai Creta is better for families who want a spacious, feature-loaded SUV with a premium interior. The Tata Nexon is better for safety-conscious buyers (5-star GNCAP) who want value for money. Creta is more comfortable for highway drives, while Nexon is more fun to drive in the city.
Is Tata Nexon safer than Hyundai Creta? +
Yes, the Tata Nexon scored 5 stars in Global NCAP crash tests while the Hyundai Creta scored 3 stars. Nexon has a stronger body structure and better side impact protection. If safety is your top priority, Nexon is the clear winner.
Which has better mileage Creta or Nexon? +
The Tata Nexon diesel gives approximately 23-24 kmpl while the Hyundai Creta diesel gives about 21-22 kmpl. In petrol, both deliver around 16-18 kmpl in real-world conditions. Nexon has slightly better fuel efficiency across variants.
What is the price difference between Creta and Nexon? +
The Tata Nexon starts at approximately Rs 8 lakh (ex-showroom) while the Hyundai Creta starts at approximately Rs 11 lakh. The top-end Nexon costs about Rs 15 lakh compared to the top-end Creta at Rs 20 lakh. Nexon offers better value at every price point.
