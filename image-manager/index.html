How to add school photos

  1. Select a school from the dropdown below
  2. Open Google Images in another tab and search for the school name
  3. Right-click on a good image and select "Copy image address"
  4. Paste the URL in the box below and click "Add Image"
  5. Repeat for 2-3 images per school
  6. Click "Save Images" to update the school page
