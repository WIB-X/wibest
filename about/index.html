About WIB

WIB is India's unified comparison platform. We help people make better decisions across every major life choice — from schools and colleges to electronics, cars, and places to live.

We believe comparison data should be free, transparent, and independent. No sponsored listings, no paid placements, no hidden agendas.

Our Values

Independence

No school, college, or brand pays us to rank them higher. Data determines rankings.

Transparency

Every data point is sourced from public information or community reviews. Nothing is hidden.

Accessibility

Free for everyone. No paywalls, no premium tiers for basic comparison data.

Community

Reviews from real parents, students, and users. No fake reviews, no paid testimonials.
