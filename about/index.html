About WIB

India's unified comparison platform. One destination for every comparison you need.

Our mission

WIB was built to solve a simple problem: there is no single place in India where you can compare everything that matters — schools, colleges, electronics, cars, places to live — with consistent, verified data.

We are building that platform. Data-driven. Independent.
